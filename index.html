Privacy Policy for AxiaCV

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Axiagigs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AxiaCV mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We provide various features to assist your career development. To provide these services, we process certain information:

  • Resume Content: Resume Analyzer and CV Builder content is processed to provide AI feedback.
  • Interview Audio & Transcription: Spoken answers are converted to text and analyzed by AI. Raw audio files are not stored.
  • Job Tracking Data: Job Tracker information is stored locally on your device using Room Database.
  • Camera Feed: Used only for interview practice visual preview and is never recorded or transmitted.

2. Third-Party AI Processing (Google Gemini)

AxiaCV uses Google's Gemini API to provide resume analysis and interview feedback.

  • Data submitted for AI analysis is transmitted securely.
  • Axiagigs does not use submitted content to train its own AI models.
  • AI features depend on internet connectivity and third-party service availability.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Job application history remains on your device.
  • No Cloud Accounts: AxiaCV currently does not require user accounts.
  • Future Features: Future cloud features will be accompanied by updates to this policy.

4. Data Deletion

Users may delete locally stored data by uninstalling the application or clearing application data through device settings.

5. AI-Generated Content Disclaimer

AxiaCV provides AI-generated career guidance, resume feedback, interview coaching, and recommendations. While we strive for accuracy, AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies or suggestions that are not suitable for every situation.

Users remain solely responsible for reviewing and verifying recommendations.

Axiagigs does not guarantee employment outcomes, interview success, job offers, salary increases, or career advancement.

6. Permissions

  • CAMERA – Interview simulation visual preview.
  • RECORD_AUDIO – Speech-to-text interview analysis.

7. Children's Privacy

AxiaCV is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
